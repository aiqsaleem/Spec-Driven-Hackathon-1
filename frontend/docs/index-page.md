### For Beginners
Start with Chapter 1 to understand the fundamental ROS 2 concepts before moving to Chapters 2 and 3.

### For Experienced Developers
Skip to specific chapters based on your needs, but review Chapter 1 if you're new to ROS 2's architecture.

### For Robotics Researchers
Focus on the integration patterns in Chapter 2 and the URDF modeling in Chapter 3 for building complex robotic systems.
